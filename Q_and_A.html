Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is the purpose of this website?

This website serves as a diet tracking application designed to help individuals lead healthier lives by providing an intuitive platform for tracking daily meals, caloric intake, and nutritional balance.

Question 2: How can I track my meals?

You can log meals by entering details of the food items you've consumed for breakfast, lunch, and dinner into the respective sections of your meal planner.

Question 3: How do I access my profile?

To access your profile, click on the "Profile" link in the navigation menu at the top of the page.

Question 4: What is the privacy policy of this website?

Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. You can find it in the footer or by contacting customer service.

Question 5: How do I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service through the "Contact Us" page on the website or by using the "Customer Service" link in the main menu.

Question 6: How do I report a bug on the website?

To report a bug, please use the "Contact Us" page to send us detailed information about the issue you encountered.

Question 7: Can I share my progress with friends?

While the platform doesn't have a direct sharing feature, you can share your progress manually by taking screenshots or through the sharing options on your dashboard.

Question 8: Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, there is no dedicated mobile app, but you can access the full functionality of the diet diary through your mobile browser.

Question 9: What happens if I don't log my meals?

While it's optional, logging your meals is beneficial for tracking your dietary intake accurately and receiving personalized recommendations.

Question 10: What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and PayPal, to make your payments convenient and secure.

Question 11: How do I get started with the diet planner?

To get started, create your account, set your dietary goals, and start logging your daily meals to track your progress.

Question 12: What is the best way to lose weight?

A balanced diet combined with regular exercise is the best way to lose weight effectively and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
