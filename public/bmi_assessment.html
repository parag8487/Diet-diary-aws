Underweight (BMI < 18.5)

A BMI below 18.5 indicates insufficient body weight for height. This may suggest malnutrition, eating disorders, or other health issues. Consultation with a healthcare professional is recommended.

Normal Weight (18.5 - 24.9)

A BMI in this range is considered healthy for most adults. It suggests a balance between height and weight associated with lower health risks.

Overweight (25 - 29.9)

A BMI in this range indicates excess body weight. It may increase health risks for conditions like heart disease and diabetes. Lifestyle changes are often recommended.

Obese (30+)

A BMI of 30 or higher indicates significant excess body fat. This category is associated with increased risk of serious health conditions. Medical advice is strongly recommended.
